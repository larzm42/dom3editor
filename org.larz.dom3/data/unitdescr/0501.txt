<p>The Allfather is an ancient Aesir. He is a master of magic and a great general. Like his subjects, he is a master of illusion and is able to pass undetected through enemy lands. Like the Vanjarls of Vanheim, the Allfather is able to cross the oceans by ship. The Allfather is at all times accompanied by his great eight-legged horse who is able to ride the winds.</p>
