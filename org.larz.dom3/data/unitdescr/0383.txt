<p>An undead demon prince called or sent by the Lord of the Underworld, he is winged and shrouded in death, and will annihilate the living of this world to make it a realm of the dead. The Prince of Death is physically strong and a master of Death magic. His mere presence causes the dead to leave their graves and follow him. His only weakness is his unnatural state of existence, which makes it possible for priests to banish him from this world.</p>
