<p>The Bandar are large, black apes who tower over their fellow apes and monkeys. The colour of their caste is red, the colour of blood and war. The Bandar are the warrior caste and although few in number, command much influence. The king and nobility are always Bandar. Swords are considered the most noble of all weapons and are only given to individuals who have the right parents or have made themselves names as skilled warriors. To be given a sword is a sure way to advance in society and to get enemies. With the sword comes the duty to protect the royalty.</p>
