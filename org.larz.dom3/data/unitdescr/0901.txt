<p>The awakening of the Vengeful Woods has altered the very essence of Pangaea's inhabitants. Dryads with black hides are born, mirroring the dark mood of the forests. These Black Dryads serve the Dark God of the Woods as the White Dryads served Mother Nature in ancient times. They have lost some of the religious powers of the white dryads and their beauty-inspired aura of awe has completely vanished.</p>
