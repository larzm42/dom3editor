<p>The Mother of Monsters is an ancient being that has given birth to serpent-kin, monsters and false gods for ages. She is a hideous being with the upper torso of a human female and the lower body of a great serpent. She is a powerful and versatile mage skilled in the paths of Nature and Death. The Mother of Monsters is always surrounded by noxious fumes and she is naturally resistant to poison. As the mother of serpent-kin, she sacred to lamias and will receive three additional lamias with each summoning.</p>
