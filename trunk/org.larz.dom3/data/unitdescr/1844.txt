<p>Scathach is a warrior sorceress, a descendant of the Morrigans. On her hidden isle of dread mists she initiates young heroes in the art of war and lovemaking. Scathach has the martial skills of a Morrigan and sorcerous powers of the Partholonians. She has prophetic powers and has divined the eventual demise of her every student. She wears a raven cloak wich allows her to fly and wields a horrible spear of the Morrigans.</p>
